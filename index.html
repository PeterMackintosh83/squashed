Club Information


Squashed is a local squash club for the Aberdeen / Aberdeenshire area which was founded in 2015 and is open to all genders and ages from 6yrs of age and upwards.

Squashed plays out of Aberdeen Sports Village (ASV) located in the city of Aberdeen. ASV is the premier sport and exercise facility in Scotland containing four commonwealth grade courts and viewing gallery.

The club started as a group of 6 friends who would regularly play together but has now swollen its ranks to contain over 50 enthusiastic active members.

Squashed have junior and adult teams representing the club at national level with asperations of returning victorious and providing the club with its first taste of silverware.
