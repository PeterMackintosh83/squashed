Club Information

Squashed is a local squash club for the Aberdeenshire area, squashed was founded in 2015 and is open to all genders and ages from 6yrs old upwards.

Squashed plays out of Aberdeen Sports Village located in the city of Aberdeen.

The club started as a group of 6 friends who would regularly play together but has now swollen its ranks to contain over 50 enthusiastic active members.

Squashed have junior and adult teams representing the club at national level with asperations of returning victorious and providing the club with its first taste of silverware.
