Club Information

Squashed is a local squash club for the Aberdeenshire area founded in 2010.

Squashed plays out of Garioch Sports Center located in Inverurie.

The club started as a group of 6 friends who would regularly play together but has now swollen its ranks to contain over 50 active enthusiastic members.

Squashed have a team representing the club at national level.
