Ability definitions below to aid selection criteria:

Novice:-

Basic understanding of rules, concepts and positioning on the court.

Basic ability to return a shot and perform a rally (sequence of back and forth shots).

Proficient:-

General understanding of rules, concepts and positioning on the court.

Ability to maintain a rally of 10 shots +

Competant to perform a range of shots (forehand, backhand, serve, drop, smash etc...).

Expert:-

Full understanding of rules, concepts and positioning on the court.

Experiance of competing in league/tournament competion.
